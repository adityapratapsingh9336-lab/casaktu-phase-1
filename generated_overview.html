Centre for Advanced Studies is an in-campus research driven institute established by Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Technical University Lucknow to impart state of the art education to post graduate students and to facilitate quality research work in the emerging areas of Engineering and Technology.

The Institute targets impactful research and innovations in the broad areas of Computer Science and Engineering, Mechatronics, Nanotechnology, Manufacturing Technology and Automation, and Energy Science and Technology.

The Institute is well equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories like Machine Learning Lab installed with Nvidia GPUs, Cybercity Lab for working on cyber security threats in a smart city environment, Google Developers Codelabs established by Google, IoT Lab, Linux Lab, Sensors drives and control Lab, 3D printing Lab with the facilities of Fused Deposition Modeling and Multi Jet printing, Industrial Automation Lab for facilitate complete automation, Industrial robotic center, Design and simulation center, Tribology Lab, Materials Chemistry and Synthesis Laboratory, Micro and Nano Characterization facility, Nano fabrication facility, Device testing facility, Electrochemical Energy Storage Lab, Battery testing facility, Photovoltaics and solar energy lab, and embedded system lab. These facilities provide for research and innovations in interdisciplinary areas of Engineering and Technology to the interested faculty and students of the University promoting growth and progress of the Institute through collaboration by offering training, internships, short term courses, and research projects.

Rewards

The Institute recognises meritorious students with a gold medal for Best M.Tech. Dissertation in each department and a cash prize for research publication in an SCI indexed journal every academic year.
