Director's Message

Welcome to the Centre for Advanced Studies

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the Centre for Advanced Studies (CAS), a premier research and innovation hub at AKTU. Our mission is to foster a culture of excellence in emerging technologies and interdisciplinary research. We are dedicated to producing thought leaders and highly skilled professionals who can tackle global technological challenges.

At CAS, we bridge the gap between academia and industry through state-of-the-art laboratories, global partnerships, and an uncompromising commitment to academic rigor.

Our Mission

Our Vision

To emerge as a globally recognized center of excellence in technological innovation, sustainable research, and advanced learning.

Our Objectives

To build strong industry-academia linkages, incubate startups, and cultivate an ecosystem of lifelong learning and ethical practices.
