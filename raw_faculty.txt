He previously served as an Assistant Professor at the Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology Bhopal (MANIT Bhopal) for the academic year 2024-25, where he actively contributed to undergraduate and postgraduate teaching, curriculum development in physics. Dr. Maurya obtained his Ph.D. in Experimental Condensed Matter Physics from IISER Bhopal in 2024. He completed his M.Sc. in Physics from BHU, Varanasi in 2018, and his B.Sc. in Physics from MJPRU Bareilly in 2016. His research focuses on the synthesis and optical characterization of nanomaterials, and on studying their optoelectronic properties using photoluminescence (PL), absorption spectroscopy, and transient absorption (TA) spectroscopy, as well as nonlinear optical phenomena such as optical limiting and Q-switching. His broader interests include ultrafast carrier dynamics, photoinduced charge transfer processes, and optoelectronic device applications. He has published 8 research articles in reputed SCI-indexed international journals, including high-impact journals, along with 3 conference papers, and is actively involved in interdisciplinary research projects and national–international collaborations focusing on next-generation optoelectronic technologies. Dr. Maurya has delivered presentations at prominent national and international conferences, earning recognition among his peers. In recognition of his academic and research excellence, Dr. Maurya has received several prestigious awards and fellowships, including CSIR-NET Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) with All India Rank (AIR 206), SERB Travel Grant (2024), Best Oral Presentation Award (FPAS 2024), Fellowship for Training of Young Scientists (MP-CST, 2022), Incubic Milton Chang Travel Grant (CLEO 2021, USA), INSPIRE Fellowship (DST, 2019), INSPIRE Scholarship (DST, 2014), and the Top 1% in Board Examination Award (UP Board, 2013).

She is a Subject Matter Expert on the TCS-iON (Tata Consultancy Services – Integrated Online Network) panel. Previously, she has served as Subject Matter Expert in Professional Communication at the Faculty of Engineering and Technology, University of Lucknow (2023–2025), and as Assistant Professor (Professional Communication) at the same institution (2017–2019), where she pioneered the establishment of a Language Lab and integrated "Moral Values and Professional Ethics" into the B.Tech curriculum. She has also held teaching positions at Biyani Girl's College, Jaipur (2012–2016) and BBD National Institute of Technology and Management, Lucknow (2005–2006). She has authored nine peer-reviewed journal articles published in national and international journals including RJELAL, IJELTS, Pune Research, and International Journal for Research Trends and Innovation. Her research areas encompass English literature, diaspora studies, professional and technical communication, cross-cultural communication, and the intersection of digital media with language pedagogy. She has presented papers at national conferences, including a recent presentation on "Integrating IKS into Professional Communication: A Holistic Ethical Shift" at Khwaja Moinuddin Chisti Language University, Lucknow (2025), and served as an Organizing Committee Member for the International Conference on "Recent Advances in Applied Science and Humanities in Evolution of Engineering" at the Faculty of Engineering and Technology, University of Lucknow (2025). Dr. Singh is a Life Member of the Association for English Studies of India and is recognized for her exemplary communication skills, collaborative leadership, and sustained commitment to institutional excellence. She has demonstrated adaptability in navigating evolving pedagogical landscapes, from traditional to technology-enabled teaching, and has contributed significantly to enhancing student engagement, career readiness, and institutional culture through her leadership roles as Convenor of the Cultural Club and Training & Placement Cell (2017–2019).
